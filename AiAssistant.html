Why Strong Passwords Matter

A strong password is one of the most powerful defenses against online harassment, impersonation, hacking, and unauthorized access to your digital accounts. Women in public life, activism, leadership, and online spaces are especially targeted by coordinated attacks ‚Äî often starting with weak or reused passwords.

Prevent Account Takeovers

Harassers often try to break into social media or email accounts to post harmful content, leak private data, or impersonate victims. A strong password makes these attacks extremely difficult.

Stop Impersonation Attempts

When your accounts are secure, attackers cannot pretend to be you online ‚Äî reducing the chances of misinformation, character attacks, or deepfake distribution.

Reduce Doxxing Risks

Hackers often access personal information through weak logins. Strong passwords help protect your private data, location, contacts, and sensitive files.

Build Digital Confidence

Knowing your accounts are fully secure empowers you to participate more safely in public conversations, leadership roles, and online engagement.
